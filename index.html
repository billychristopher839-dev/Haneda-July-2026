Crew Guide · You've got this

How to Play (and why you'll love it)

Hello, Director! 👋 Think of this game as a friendly co-pilot for your real life. Your everyday to-do list becomes a flight schedule, and every task you finish helps a real airport — Haneda — climb toward the top of the world. No pressure, just progress. Here's everything you need to know.

✈️ The 4 simple steps

  • 1. Plan. Go to Daily Ops, tap any day in July, and add your tasks. Set whatever time fits your day — early, late, or no time at all. Nothing is forced to a default.
  • 2. Fly. As you finish each task in real life, tap its box to check it off. Watch your day fill up.
  • 3. Close out. Hit Close out the day to lock in your score based on how much you completed.
  • 4. Climb. Haneda begins dead last (#31 of 31 hubs). Every closed-out day opens a new route and lifts you up the world rankings — but only steady, consistent days will carry you to #1. 🏆

🎯 How scoring works

Score is based on how much of your planned schedule you complete that day:

You completed…StatusPoints
100% of your schedulePerfect Departure ✦16 pts
75% or moreOn Schedule8 pts

A gentle reminder: a low-scoring day is never a failure — even the world's best airports have foggy mornings and delays. Reopen any day to edit it, log tomorrow, and keep flying. You can always replay the whole season too. The goal is steady momentum, not perfection. 💛
